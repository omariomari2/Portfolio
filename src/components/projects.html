1. Introducing MSUnit

Python Django SQLite Render PostgreSQL

MSUnit is an innovative web-based clinical management system designed to transform stroke patient care. Our platform empowers medical professionals with real-time tracking, intelligent alerting, and seamless communication, ensuring that every second counts in critical situations.

With MSUnit, healthcare providers can efficiently manage patient data, monitor vital signs, and facilitate remote consultations with neurologists. Our system features role-based access control, automated risk assessments, and comprehensive clinical documentation, all while adhering to HIPAA compliance standards.

We aim to revolutionize stroke care by reducing response times, minimizing documentation errors, and creating a streamlined workflow for stroke unit management. Experience the future of healthcare with MSUnit, where technology meets compassion for better patient outcomes.

5. CosmicVista

Designed as a hands-on learning tool, this project helps younger students visualize and engage with space in a more immersive way than traditional methods allow. Future expansions aim to simulate real-time cosmic events and deeper space phenomena, making CosmicVista a continuously evolving space learning platform.
